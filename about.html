About JLSC

At the Varicose Vein Clinic you can expect to receive a thorough evaluation of your condition which includes duplex ultrasound examination to assess the state and function of your veins. We will then discuss treatment options, presenting the advantages, disadvantages and possible risks of various procedures. Various treatment options will include surgery, sclerotherapy laser and radio frequency ablation or a combination of other modalities to deal most effectively with veins of different sizes.

Jordan laser surgery center at Al-Ahli Hospital in Amman was newly established and equipped with the latest diagnostic equipment and treatment devices like the acoustic colored ECHO Doppler, photosynthesis detector, optical vision devices, radio frequency, diode laser devices and general / local anesthesia.

At JLSC we believe careful and accurate examination of the problem using the latest technology with the experience of Dr Jaafreh will allow us to provide with the most appropriate treatment plan. This will be sure to provide the best results.

All of that is under the management and supervision of Dr. Ibrahim Ja'afreh, a consultant surgeon and a member of the American College of Phlebology (for the treatment of varicose veins).

Please make yourself familiar with our site and find out more about each service available for you. Please contact us and we will provide you with the professional service you expect in a private and comfortable atmosphere.

Meet Dr. Ibrahim Ja'afreh

Dr Ibrahim Ja'afreh
the head of JLSC ( Jordan Laser Surgical Center)
a consultant surgen and member of the (ACP - The American College of Phlebology.)


Dr. Ibrahim Ja'afreh graduated from Jash institute of Medicine and pharmacology ,Romania in 1982 with a Bachelor of Medicine and a Bachelor of Surgery. He holds the Jordanian Board in general surgery since 1991 .He became a member of the JMA (The Jordanian Medical Association) and a member of the JSS ( The Jordanian Surgical Society.)


After practicing as a doctor for several years in the Jordanian and Palestinian hospitals, he decided to establish his own clinic, initially practicing in Jordan and later in Palestine, where he then became the head of the Surgical department at al Mezan hospital in Palestine .

he became interested in varicose vein disease after seeing many patients suffering with no ultimate solution. And after many workshops in Holand, Italy an romani and america , he became a member of the American college of phlebology. He was one of the first physicians in Jordan to perform non-surgical closure of varicose veins using EVLT (Endovenous Laser Treatment) combined with sclerotherapy.


He is a leading expert in the field of vein disorders known as Phlebology. He understands that the first step of successful treatment is done through evaluations. A non easy procesure which he does it using the latest doplar technology to undentify the problem and stat mapping.


His experience in Laser surgical procedures ranges from common medical concerns such as wound care management to varicose veins, ulcers , piles and fistulas. Using injection sclerotherapy, endovascular laser, radio frequency, exo laser He has spent a decade researching these new effective procedures. He and his staff are proud to use the latest most advanced technology and products brought from Italy and America and belgica.


Now he offers you all of his knowledge expertise as the head of the Jordan Laser Medical center. The first center of its kind to illuminate all of your problems in an affordable price.
